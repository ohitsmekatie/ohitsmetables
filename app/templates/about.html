About This Project

🛠️ The Technology

Lore & More is a game prep resource site built with:

  • 🐍 Flask (Python) for the backend
  • ✍️ Poetry for managing dependencies
  • ✍️ Render for deployment
  • 📄 Google Sheets as a dynamic content engine
  • 🎨 Bootstrap + custom CSS for the layout
  • ✨ Font Awesome icons
🎲 About Katie

Hello! I’m Katie. I'm a game lover based in Pittsburgh, where I live with my husband and our many many animals.

I’ve been playing and running RPGs for years, but recently have been getting more into content creation. I love making isometric maps and RPG related doodles and someday i'll upload those here as well.

I know a lot of these sites already exist, but this was a way for me to explore Flask for something I love (world building!) I hope it inspires your prep, your play, and your stories! Speaking of some of those other sites! Here are some of my favorites:

  • Don Jon. Truly inspiring how much content is on that site
  • Necrotic Gnome OSE Generators
  • The Tome of Adventure Design
