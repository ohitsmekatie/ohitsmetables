About This Project

🛠️ The Technology

Game Prep Galore is a game prep resourse built with:

  • 🐍 Flask (Python) for the backend
  • 📄 Google Sheets as a dynamic content engine
  • 🎨 Bootstrap + custom CSS for the layout
  • ✨ Font Awesome icons to make things sparkle
🎲 About Katie

Hi! I’m Katie — a GM based in Pittsburgh, where I live with my husband and our many many animals.

I’ve been playing and running RPGs for years, but recently have been getting more into content creation. I love making maps and RPG related doodles and someday i'll upload those here as well.

This site is my way of blending that love of tabletop games with a little bit of web whimsy. I hope it inspires your prep, your play, and your stories!
