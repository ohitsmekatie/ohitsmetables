A collection of tables for game prep and word building.



Hello! ohitsmetables is a resource you can use for world building in your table top role playing games. Each secion has a bunch of tables for you to use and let the dice choose your fate. The following explains each section and what's in them. If you have any questions just catch me at one of the links below.

  • Food: tables & pre-mades related to food, menus, campgfire cooking
  • Characters: tables & pre-mades related to characters, monsters, npcs
  • Locations: tables & pre-mades for crafting locations like villages, taverns
  • Things: tables & pre-mades for items, treasure, and misc. that don't fit into other categories
