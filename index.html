A collection of resources for game prep and word building.



Hello! ohitsmetables is a collection of resources i've made that you can use in your TTRPGs. Each secion has different resources. The following explains each section and what's in them. If you have any questions just catch me at one of the links below.

  • Tables: tables for generating different content in your game
  • NPCs: pre-made NPCs for you to include in your game
  • Maps: maps that you can print out and use ! for free ! in your games
  • One shots: small one shots that i've written that you can use for side quests, short sessions, whatever!
